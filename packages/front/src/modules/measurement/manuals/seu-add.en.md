- This page is used to identify and monitor Significant Energy Users (SEUs) that have a major impact on energy performance.
- Each SEU is linked to a specific energy resource (for example, electricity or gas) and related metrics.
- When creating an SEU, you can select departments or meters:
  - If you select meters manually, the SEU will only include those meters, and it will not change automatically in the future.
  - If you select departments, the SEU will automatically include all meters within those departments. When new meters are added to these departments, they will be included automatically.
- Review SEUs regularly to identify high-energy users and find improvement opportunities.

## SEU Suggestions

- The system analyzes consumption data within the **selected date and time range** and dynamically suggests potential SEUs based on high-energy meters (consuming at least 80% of total energy).
- Suggestions are automatically recalculated when the selected date or time range changes.
- Each suggestion includes a recommended name, energy resource type, and list of high-consumption meters.
- Click "Select" on any suggestion to prefill the form, then modify as needed before saving.
