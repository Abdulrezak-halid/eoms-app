- The purpose of this worksheet is to help you analyze the forces that will help and hinder your efforts to improve energy performance. There are 3 steps in developing these thoughts:
   1. Think about the factors that will assist you, we call these "significant variables." There are examples in the table above, but you need to delete them and replace them with the realities in your situation.
   2. Think about the factors that will hinder you, we call these "barriers." There are examples above, but you need to delete them and replace them with the real barriers in your situation. In many organizations, barriers are related to people.
   3. For each of the identified barriers, decide how you will reduce the strength of this barrier. Develop an action plan to mitigate the impact of each barrier.
- Give a score from 1 to 5 for each significant variable and each barrier based on their strength and impact.
- The key to improving the situation is to reduce the impact of barriers rather than trying to increase the impact of significant variables.
- Much of reducing the impact of barriers is about communication and engaging key individuals.
